Tacna Chamber of Commerce

Site plan

Site Name

The name of the site is simple and self-explanatory "Tacna Chamber of Commerce" was chosen to clearly represent the locale of Tacna and emphasize the organization's role as a chamber of commerce. This straightforward and location-specific name helps create a sense of identity and connection to the local business community.

Site Purpose

The Tacna Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to serving as a pivotal hub for trade and business development in the Tacna region. We provide essential services to our members, fostering economic growth, and facilitating trade relationships within and beyond our community.

Color schema

The primary color of the website is this shade of blue. I selected blue to evoke a sense of trust and security

White, on the other hand, is chosen to symbolize transparency and honesty.

By incorporating this color scheme, the Tacna Chamber of Commerce website will present a professional and visually appealing design, with blue providing a sense of trust and reliability, and white ensuring a clean and easy-to-read layout..

I'll add grey or dark blue.

Typography

For titles and the menu, I've decided to utilize the Domine font. This serif font conveys a sense of stability and seriousness, which aligns well with the confidence-focused nature of this business.

As for the main body of the page, including all the text, I've opted for the Open Sans font. It's a sans-serif font known for its readability and straightforward design.

If there's a need to emphasize or present something differently, I'll employ the Seaweed Script font.
